About Ahjin Guild

Founded by the legendary Shadow Monarch, Sung Jin-Woo, the Ahjin Guild stands at the forefront of humanity's defense against supernatural threats. Our mission is to maintain peace and order in a world constantly challenged by monsters and dimensional rifts.

What sets us apart:

  • Elite Hunters: Our roster includes some of the most powerful and skilled hunters in the world, led by S-Rank hunters.
  • Cutting-edge Technology: We utilize state-of-the-art equipment and magical artifacts to enhance our hunters' capabilities.
  • Global Network: With connections spanning across continents, we're able to respond swiftly to threats anywhere in the world.
  • Training Programs: We offer comprehensive training for hunters of all ranks, helping them reach their full potential.

At Ahjin Guild, we believe in the power of unity and the indomitable spirit of humanity. Join us in our quest to protect the world and push the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of hunters.
